ABB Metering Modules

ABB Metering Modules are bundled, pre-tested solutions for your fiscal metering, blending and pressure reduction applications.

They are easy to implement and ensure the measurement accuracy of oil and gas products being sold.

These solutions can be pre-fabricated, pre-assembled and pre-configured to be delivered as one single solution for easy startup or they can arrive in modules for on-site assembly and set-up.

Our modular approach helps ensure consistent quality, predictable performance, simple operation and easy maintenance.
